Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you need? Give us a call or stop by — we're happy to talk it through.

What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday through Saturday, 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Sunday 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Most tune-ups and small repairs are walk-in — no appointment needed.

Do I need an appointment for a repair?

Not for most services. Basic and Standard Tune-Ups are typically completed same-day on a walk-in basis. For a Full Overhaul or a custom build, we recommend calling ahead so we can plan the time your bike needs.

What's the difference between the three tune-up levels?

Basic covers safety and smoothness — brakes, gears, chain lube, tire pressure. Standard adds wheel truing, full cable and derailleur inspection, and bolt torque checks. Full Overhaul is a complete teardown: degreased drivetrain, frame cleaning and inspection, and a full rebuild. Use the tracker above to compare them side by side.

Do you sell new bikes, or just repairs?

Both. We carry road, city, gravel, and triathlon bikes from trusted brands, plus a full range of parts, accessories, and fitness gear. Every new bike includes a free fit check.

What if I bought my bike somewhere else?

No problem at all — we service any bike, any brand, regardless of where it was purchased. Bring it in for an inspection and we'll recommend exactly what it needs, with pricing approved by you first.

Do you work on e-bikes?

Yes — our mechanics service e-bike drivetrains, brakes, and general maintenance. For battery or motor-specific electronics issues, give us a call ahead so we can confirm we have the right parts on hand.
